# Random Hit Locations

> This post is part of [Prismatic Wasteland's Random Blogwagon][rb]

Do you need some random hit locations? No not body parts where attacks land like [this table from Retired Adventurer][ra-hl]. I'm talking exotic locales for assassination. Everyone deserves to die but some deserve to die a little sooner and by your sweaty little hands. So why not do the deed somewhere beautiful and with a bunch of ~~witnesses to kill~~ crowds to hide in.
