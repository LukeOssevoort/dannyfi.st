# Deaf Bringer

> Attempting to cure tone deafness

Magnolia Keep[^mk] has definitively killed and then kicked the metaphorical
horse that is Deathbringer, the hottest TTRPG on crowdfunding. I am not
here to just jump on the bandwagon, as fun as that has been on Discord.
See I suffer from the unfortunate disease of being able to see the
potential in things and wanting to fix it. Doubly so when the thing
that is so clearly missing is a sense of humour. Plus I keep getting
ads for this stupid thing so I need to work the sliver out of my brain
somehow.

## The Problem

Everything wrong with this game comes from it trying to have its cake
and eat it too. It clearly and desperately wants to be seen as a satirical
take on 80s chainmail bikini fantasy whilst also relishing in the male
gaze. These two positions cannot coexist; one will overtake the other
and in this case it has aired on the side of making every woman an
underwear model. No joke you can write can get you past that first
impression.

Now of course there were other issues (outlined by Magnolia Keep[^mk])
but they stem from this root. Every bad, tone deaf choice made comes
from a fundamental lack in being able to commit to the bit. It shows
in the highly rendered 5e style art, the ads showing characters in a
metal band and the confused comparisons to Dark Sun.
