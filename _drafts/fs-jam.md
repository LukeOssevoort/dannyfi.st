# Frontier Scum Jam

> Some say men are made outta mud. This time it's literal!

The town of Moresby is at a standstill. Local coal miners have gone on strike, siezed the mine and now regularly raid the town for supplies. The coal baron doesn't want to budge and the town now sits on the precipice of chaos.

- Learn how so few miners have held out for so long
- Meet the deadly mercenaries the coal baron has hired to take back his mine
- Visit Petey's Pickle Palace
- Be the deciding factor in a standoff set to change Moresby forever

**Will you lick the boot that feeds you? Will you seize the means of production through dark rites? Or will you leave the town of Moresby a muddy graveyard?**
