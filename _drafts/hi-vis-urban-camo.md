# Hi-Vis as Urban Camo

As oft stated on this blog, when I am not rolling dice or writing silly
little blogs I am an engineer. Primarily I work in Safety, Control And
Data Acquisition (SCADA); or more simply smart electrical metering and
switching. It means for one that I have worked on the modern demon that
is data centers[^dc], as well as residential buildings. These buildings
are interesting sites to do maintenance on, because from a safety
perspective hi-vis is not neccessary but if I don't building security
gets called.

[^dc]: Which is a lot of misplaced rage if you ask me, data centers
are also where all your social media posts live. The real issue VC
rapidly expanding on a technology for maximum profit rather than an
actual beneficial applications. It happened to crypto and NFTs too.
Probably should be its own blog post.
